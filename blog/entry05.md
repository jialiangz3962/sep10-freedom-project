### Content/Sources
For context, the tool that I chose for my freedom project is Bulma. The reason why I chose Bulma is because of the simple, easy to understand components that are similar to Bootstrap. While I was learning the tool, the most information that I collected was mainly from (their documentations)[https://bulma.io/documentation/], but I also learned Bulma more in depth from a video on youtube going over a (comprehensive course)[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nUsMXdZrTEI]. Things that I learned from Bulma is their grid system and alignment along with readable css such as `is-primary` and `is-large`. Things that I usually struggle with is organization, hence I chose Bulma as it's main property is to input things on wireframes and organize it to the user's liking. One section of code that I specifically like from Bulma is their html skeleton, as it looks appealing to the eye and is actually helpful in reminding me of where to place things. Here is an example of code for the html-skeleton.


I inputted the wireframe my friend and I used for SHABR and used it to demo the Bulma wireframe to myself, and it turned out as I liked it.
